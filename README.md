Receiver Radio FM With Arduino

For this project i used the following components:

- Arduino Nano;
- Radio receiver module RDA5807;
- Antenna spiral from copper;
- Display LCD 16x2;
- Adjustable resistor of 10 kohmi;
- Potentiometer B10K;
- Leds of different colors;
- Resistor of 390 ohmi;
- Breadboard;
- Female Audio Jack of 3.5 mm;
- Wires for conections between components.

For the radio module RDA5807 was realised breakout.

Conections between radio module and arduino nano:

- SDA – pinul A4;
- SCL – pinul A5;
- VDD – 3.3V;
- GND – GND.

Conections between display lcd and arduino nano:

- Pinul 1 la Ground;
- Pinul 2 la 5V;
- Pinul 3 la semireglabil;
- Pinul 4 - RS (Data/Instruction Select) la pinul digital 12;
- Pinul 5 la Ground;
- Pinul 6 - Enable la pinul digital 11;
- Pinul 11- D4 la pinul digital 5;
- Pinul 12 - D5 la pinul digital 4;
- Pinul 13 - D6 la pinul digital 3;
- Pinul 14 - D7 la pinul digital 2;
- Pinul 15 la rezistenta de 390 ohmi si pinul 16 la Ground.
